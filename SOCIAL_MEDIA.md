## Tips for Maximum Engagement

1. **Timing**: Post during peak hours (9-11 AM or 1-3 PM in your timezone)
2. **Visuals**: Include screenshots or the OGP image
3. **Engagement**: Ask a question in the thread
4. **Follow-up**: Reply to comments within the first hour
5. **Cross-post**: Share on LinkedIn, Product Hunt, Hacker News
